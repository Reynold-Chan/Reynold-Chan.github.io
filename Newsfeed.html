End of Arup Internship in Hong Kong- August 2020

I started my internship in Toronto and concludes it in Hong Kong. The pandemic has put a new perspective toward everything in life for me. I am so grateful for having this opportunity despite a world-wide pandemic going on. I learnt more than I could have ever imagined during this intership process and formed a lot of my personal identity. I hope that the future will be filled with similar experiences of personal growth.

Hong Kong Geopark- July 2020

This is my fourth time staying in Hong Kong for an extended amount of time, yet there is still so much to discover! These volcanic rock formations are really interesting.

Hiking the Lion Rock Mountain - July 2020

This is the most infamous mountain in Hong Kong, the view is stunning. I would argue that this view is much better than the view from the tourist area of Victoria Peak. However, one should not attempt this hike during the summer. At the very least, ensure that you have enough water.
